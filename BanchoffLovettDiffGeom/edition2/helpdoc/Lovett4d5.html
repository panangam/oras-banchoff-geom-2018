The window "Curve: X(t)" in this demonstration displays the curve X(t) = (cos(t), sin(t), cos(2t)/c), the point X(t0), and Tangent Vector of the curve

The window "Tangential Indicatrix" displays the path of the tangent vector on a transparent sphere.

As in earlier demos, there are "Show Color Spectrum", and "Show Entire Indicatrix" checkboxes.

The partialcurvature readout displays the total space curvature of the curve from t0_min to t0. The totalcurvature readout displays the total space curvature of the entire curve.

Fenchel's Theorem tells us that the total curvature must be at least 2π.
