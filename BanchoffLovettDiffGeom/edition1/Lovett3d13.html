This demo includes a single graphical window which displays the curve X(t), the point X(t0), the position vector X(t0), the tangent vector T(t0) with its tail at X(t0), and the unit sphere centered at the origin if the "Show sphere" checkbox is checked. Note that the default curve lies on the sphere and that for any curve lying on the sphere, the tangent vector T(t0) is always perpendicular to the position vector X(t0).

You can change t0 and X(t) and choose whether or not to show the entire curve and the unit sphere in the control window.
