The "Domain" window in this demonstration displays a domain as a wireframe, a point (u0, v0) in the domain, and the u-constant and v-constant curves, called the u-coordinate and v-coordinate curves, through that point in red and green, respectively. The "Surface X(u,v)" window shows the surface X(u,v) for the given domain and the images of the point and the curves from the "Domain" window. It is possible to change u0, v0, and X(u,v) in the control window. It is also possible to enter any of the functions listed in the control window for X(u,v), and to change the parameters used to define these functions.
