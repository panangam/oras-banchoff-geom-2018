This demo includes three graphical display windows.

The window "Parametric Curve and Velocity Vector" displays the curve X(t), the point X(t0), and the velocity vector X'(t0) with its tail at X(t0).

The window "Graph of speed s'(t)" displays the graph of the speed s'(t) = |X'(t)| and the area under the curve which gives the integral of s'(t), or the arclength of X(t).

The window "Graph of arclength s(t)" displays the graph of the arclength s(t) given by the integral of the function s'(t) graphed in the "Graph of speed s'(t)" window.

You can change t0 and X(t) and choose whether or not to show the entire curve in the control window. The control window includes a list of functions which you can use for X(t), along with parameters used to define those functions.
