The "Domain" window in this demonstration displays a domain as a wireframe, a point (u0, v0) in the domain, and the u-constant and v-constant curves, called the u-coordinate and v-coordinate curves, through that point in red and green, respectively. The "Surface X(u,v)" window shows the torus of revolution X(u,v) for the given domain and the images of the point and the curves from the "Domain" window. It is possible to change u0, v0, a, and b in the control window.
