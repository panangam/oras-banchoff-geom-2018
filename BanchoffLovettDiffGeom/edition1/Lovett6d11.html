The "Domain" window in this demonstration displays a domain, a point (u0, v0) in the domain, a subset of the domain colored white, red, blue, and magenta, and a point on the boundary of this subset. The "Surface: X(u, v)" window shows the sphere X(u, v) = (cos(u)cos(v), sin(u)cos(v), sin(v)) and the images of (u0, v0) and the colored subset of the domain on this surface (with each point in the domain mapped to a point of the same color). The "Normal Image" window shows the surface produced by mapping each point on the surface X(u,v) to its unit normal vector, preserving the coloring from the "Surface" window. It is possible to change the point (u0, v0) and the colored subset of the domain by clicking and dragging the points shown in the "Domain" window.
